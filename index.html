Creative Frontend Interaction Experiment

Mechanical Audio

Read More

The Breakdown

Mechanical Audio is a small frontend interaction experiment inspired by old mechanical film reels and tactile audio systems.

The project explores how rotational gestures, motion, and interaction speed can influence synchronized audio playback and visual movement in real time. Built mainly for creative exploration, the experiment focuses on making digital interaction feel more physical, reactive, and cinematic through motion-driven controls and looping film-style visuals.

Interaction Features

  • —Rotational Gesture Controls
  • —Velocity-Based Playback
  • —Infinite Film Animation
  • —Dynamic Synchronization

  • —SVG Interaction Logic
