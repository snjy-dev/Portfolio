# SNJY. — Creative Technologist Portfolio

> "Jack of all trades, master of none, but oftentimes better than master of one."

This repository contains the core frontend architecture and interaction systems for my personal signature portfolio — a cinematic, motion-driven digital experience exploring the intersection of engineering logic, minimal design, and immersive storytelling.

---

## 🎬 The Vision

The portfolio is designed as an editorial-style digital experience rather than a traditional developer website. It prioritizes typography, intentional motion, cinematic pacing, and luxury-minimalist aesthetics to present technical systems not just as projects, but as interactive digital stories.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

### Core

* HTML5
* Tailwind CSS

### Motion & Interaction

* GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform)
* ScrollTrigger
* Magnetic Interactions
* Custom Invert Cursor

### Typography & Design

* Inter
* Alex Brush (Signature Fallback)
* Figma
* Canva
* CapCut

---

## 🏗️ Selected Systems

The portfolio features a fullscreen horizontal showcase highlighting three core architectural concepts:

### 1. ParkLink AI

AI-assisted smart community parking ecosystem focused on accessibility, route-aware parking, and hands-free interaction.

### 2. Smart Queue Guardian

AI-assisted queue and crowd management system concept designed for public-service accessibility and congestion reduction.

### 3. LedgerGuard AI

Rule-based banking reconciliation prototype focused on deterministic validation, compliance logic, and explainable workflows.

---

## 🧪 Creative Labs

Beyond primary systems, the **Creative Experiments** section explores tactile and motion-driven digital interactions.

### Mechanical Audio

A frontend interaction experiment inspired by vintage film systems, exploring rotational gesture controls and velocity-based media synchronization.
